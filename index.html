Welcome to Bliss Remains

Bliss Remains is a band from Indonesia that was formed in 2021. The band consists of four members:

  • Fairus (Vocals)
  • Mushab (Guitar)
  • Tegar (Bass)
  • Abyan (Drums)
Bliss Remains is known for their unique blend of Alt rock and Modern Post-Hardcore, with catchy Riffs and heartfelt lyrics. They have released one album and several singles, gaining a loyal fanbase both in Indonesia and internationally. Their music often explores themes of personal struggles, hope, and resilience, resonating with listeners of all ages.
